# Current notes:
Open with a hooked value prop (what you do + how it helps them), not “I’m excited…”.

Add 1–2 quantified wins (time saved, latency ↓, coverage ↑, users impacted).

Show why this company (products, teams, problems) in 1 sentence with specifics.

Close with a crisp ask (“happy to share X / chat about Y”), not a generic thank-you.
